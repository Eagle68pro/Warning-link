How to correctly make cereal

  • Select a bowl of appropriate size.
  • Add cereal to the bowl first.
  • Pour milk slowly until desired coverage is achieved.
  • Eat promptly to maintain crunch.
Advanced cereal techniques
  • Cold milk preserves texture best.
  • Refill cereal, not milk, to avoid sogginess.
  • Multiple cereals may be combined at your own risk.
